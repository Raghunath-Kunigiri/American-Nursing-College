OUR COURSES

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc Nursing)

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc Nursing) program aims to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of nursing theory and practice while developing critical thinking, leadership, and communication skills. With a focus on ethical practice and lifelong learning, BSc Nursing graduates are poised to address evolving healthcare needs, advocate for patients' rights, and contribute positively to the healthcare system.

General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM)

The purpose of GNM nursing courses is to equip students with essential skills needed for diverse healthcare roles, including patient care, medication administration, and medical equipment management. Through practical training and theoretical knowledge, GNM programs prepare individuals to meet the evolving demands of the healthcare sector, ensuring quality care delivery and contributing positively to societal well-being.
