Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for BSc Nursing?

Candidates must have completed 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with minimum 50% marks. Minimum age should be 17 years and maximum 35 years.

What is the duration of the GNM program?

The General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) program is of 3.5 years duration, which includes 6 months of internship.

Are there scholarship opportunities available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding students and need-based financial assistance for economically disadvantaged students.

What is the placement record of the college?

We maintain a 98% placement record with tie-ups with leading hospitals and healthcare institutions across India and internationally.
